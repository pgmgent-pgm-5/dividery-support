Creating a new Group

Before creating a new Group, log in to your Account on the DiviDery website.

To create a Group

  1. Click the + button on the menu bar.

    The Create a Group form displays.

  2. Enter a name for your Group in the Group Name text box.
  3. (Optional) Link an image to your group by entering an image URL in the Image URL (https://) text box.
  4. Click Create.

    The new Group’s details display.

    5. The easiest way to enter an avatar URL to right-click an image you like on the internet and copy the image link address. Then simply paste the copied link into the text box. Use a square image for best results!
