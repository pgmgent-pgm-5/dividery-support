Viewing your total balance

Your total balance is the sum of what you owe and are owed across all your DiviDery Groups.

To view your total balance

  1. Click the Profile icon on the menu bar.

    The Profile tab opens.

  2. Click Balance.

    Your balance details display with totals for what you owe, are owed, and a net sum.

  3. (Optional) Click Overview for a visualization of your spending over the last month.

    A bar graph displays with totals for each bill category.
