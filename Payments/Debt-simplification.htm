Debt simplification

 

Sometimes, DiviDery will prompt you to pay a Group member that you don’t owe money to. This is an example of DiviDery’s debt simplification feature. If several Group members owe each other money, DiviDery uses debt simplification to make payments more efficient.

For example, if you owe member 1 money and member 1 also owes member 2 money, DiviDery makes pay member 2 directly. This feature lets you settle both debts with one less transaction.

With DiviDery’s debt simplification, your Group can settle debts more efficiently.

Debt simplification only works within individual DiviDery Groups. You cannot simplify debts between separate Groups.
