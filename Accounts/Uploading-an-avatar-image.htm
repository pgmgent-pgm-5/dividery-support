Linking an avatar image

Your avatar represents you on DiviDery to your Group members, so choose your avatar image wisely!

To link an avatar image

  1. Click the Profile icon in the menu bar.

    The Profile tab opens.

  2. Click the Avatar image.

    A window opens asking you to enter a URL for your image.

    The easiest way to enter an avater URL is to right-click an image you like on the internet and copy the image link address. Then simply paste the copied link into the text box. Use a square image for best results!

  3. Enter the URL of your chosen image and click OK.

The chosen image is now your new avatar!
