Deleting your Account

You want to delete your DiviDery Account? We’ll be sad to see you leave!

To delete your Account

  1. Log in to your Account on the DiviDery website.
  2. Click the Profile icon in the DiviDery menu.

    The Profile tab opens.

  3. Click the Settings icon (a small gear).

    The Settings menu displays.

  4. Click Delete Account.

    A confirmation window displays asking whether you’re sure you want to delete your Account.

    If you delete your Account, all your data including Groups, bills, and transactions will be removed from our servers. This action cannot be undone!

  5. Click Yes to confirm you want to delete your Account.

Your account and all your data, including Groups, bills, and transactions, are deleted.
