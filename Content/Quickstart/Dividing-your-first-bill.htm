Dividing your first bill

Now that you’ve created a new Group, it’s time to add a bill!

To add a bill

  1. Click the Groups icon on the menu bar.

    The Groups tab opens.

  2. Click a Group.

    The Group’s details display.

  3. Click Add a bill.

    The Create a bill form displays.

  4. Enter a name for the bill in the Name for the bill text box, and a monetary amount in the Amount text box.
  5. Select the bill category from the Select a Category dropdown menu.
  6. Keep the next dropdown for the bill splitting method to the default Equal setting.
  7. Click Split Bill.

    The Send payment invitations form displays.

  8. Click Send payment notifications after double-checking to make sure the bill is split equally.

    DiviDery sends your Group members an email notification about the new amount they owe for the bill. The bill is also added to the Group details.

Congratulations! You’ve divided your first bill in DiviDery!
