Quickstart Guide

Welcome to DiviDery!

DiviDery allows you to split bills among roommates, friends, or family without the hassle of trying to do math in your head, or remembering what you owe. DiviDery even reminds your friends if they owe you money without any awkward confrontations!

This quickstart guide will get you started with DiviDery in no time. You’ll learn how to:

  • Set up your Account
  • Create your first Group
  • Add your first bill

Then you’ll be ready to learn more by exploring the DiviDery app and the rest of this manual!
