Settling debts

If you owe money to one of your Group members, you can easily settle the debt with DiviDery

If DiviDery prompts you to pay a different Group member than you expected, it is likely simplifying your debts automatically. See Debt-simplification.htm#id21B4F0I0YVZ for more information.

To settle a debt

  1. Click the Groups icon on the menu bar.

    The Groups tab opens.

  2. Click a Group.

    The Group’s details display.

  3. Click one of the Group’s bills where you have debts owing.

    The bill’s details display.

  4. Click Pay next to a member you owe a debt to.

After a 24-hour period, your money transfers to the member you settle the debt with. Your Group and total balance automatically adjust to reflect the payment.
