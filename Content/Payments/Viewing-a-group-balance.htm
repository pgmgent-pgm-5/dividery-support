Viewing a Group balance

Too find how much you owe in a Group, you can view your current balance within each DiviDery Group that you’re a member of.

To view a Group balance

  1. Click the Groups icon on the menu bar.

    The Groups tab opens.

  2. Click a Group from the list.

    The Group details display.

The Group details include your total balance in the Group and your outstanding debts for each Group bill.
