Viewing Group activity

You can access the transaction history of all members in your DiviDery Groups from your Activities tab.

To view all Group activity

  1. Click the Activities icon on the menu bar.

    The Activities tab opens.

  2. Click All.

    A combined list of all transactions in your DiviDery Groups displays.

  3. (Optional) Click the Filter dropdown menu and select a Group to see transactions from only that Group.
