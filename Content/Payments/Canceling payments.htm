Canceling payments

You can easily cancel payments in DiviDery.

You must cancel a paid dept within 24 hours. After 24 hours, the transaction fully processes and you can no longer cancel it.

To cancel a payment

  1. Click the Activities icon in the menu bar.

    The Activities tab opens.

  2. Click Yours.

    A list of your past transactions displays.

  3. Click the transaction you want to cancel.

    The payment details display.

  4. Click Cancel Payment.

Your payment is canceled.
