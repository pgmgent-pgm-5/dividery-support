Removing Group members

DiviDery allows you to remove Group members at any time.

To remove a Group member

  1. Click the Groups icon from the menu bar.

    The Groups tab opens.

  2. Click a Group.

    The Group’s details display.

  3. Click Delete Member (garbage can icon) next the Group member you want to delete.

    The member is removed from the Group.

Removed Group members receive an email notifying them of their removal and will no longer have access to the Group’s details.
