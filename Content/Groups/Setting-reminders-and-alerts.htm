Sending reminders

If Group members are not paying their part of a bill, you can always send them a friendly reminder through DiviDery.

To send a reminder to Group member

  1. Click the Groups icon on the menu bar.

    The Groups tab opens.

  2. Click a Group.

    The Group’s details display.

  3. Click a bill with outstanding debts.

    The bill’s details display.

  4. Click Reminder next the Group member you want to send a reminder to.

DiviDery sends an automated email notification reminding the member to pay their part of the bill.
